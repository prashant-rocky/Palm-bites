Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

At Palm Bites, accessible from https://palmbites.us, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

If you have any questions or concerns about our policy or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at info@palmbites.us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Make a reservation
  • Join our mailing list
  • Contact us through forms
  • Place a catering or takeout order (if applicable)
  • Browse our website (cookies and analytics)

Types of Information Collected:

  • Personal Info: Name, email, phone, reservation date/time, guest count
  • Payment Info: Processed securely through third-party services
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser, location, device type
  • Other: Feedback, reviews, and messages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Manage reservations and dining services
  • Send promotional emails or updates (with consent)
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third parties such as:

  • Service providers (email, analytics, reservations, payments)
  • Authorities when required by law
  • In case of business transfers or mergers

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but it may affect functionality.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct or delete your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise your rights, email us at privacy@palmbites.us.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and safe payment gateways. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. If we discover we’ve collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party services (e.g., social media, delivery). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted here with the updated date.
